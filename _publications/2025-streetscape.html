--- layout: publication authors: - Areen Khalaila - Gianna Everette - Suho Kim - Ian Roy arxiv: "2503.21897" html: https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2503.21897 pdf: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2503.21897 tags: - accessibility - research - disability - visual impairment - tactile - gamification title: "StreetScape: Gamified Tactile Interactions for Collaborative Learning and Play" type: - Paper venue: CHI LBW venue_location: Yokohama, Japan venue_tags: - CHI venue_url: https://chi2025.acm.org year: 2025 pub_date: 2025-04-26 --- StreetScape is a modular tactile street puzzle designed to promote spatial reasoning and collaborative play between blind or visually impaired and sighted children. By merging gamified tactile interaction with inclusive design, it reimagines assistive technology as a playful, social, and educational tool.
