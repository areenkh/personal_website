--- layout: publication authors: - Areen Khalaila - Gianna Everette - Suho Kim - Ian Roy doi: 10.48550/arXiv.2503.21897 link: https://doi.org/10.48550/arXiv.2503.21897 html: https://arxiv.org/html/2503.21897v1 pdf: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2503.21897 tags: - accessibility - research - disability - visual impairment - tactile - gamification title: "StreetScape: Gamified Tactile Interactions for Collaborative Learning and Play" type: - Paper venue: arXiv venue_url: https://arxiv.org/abs/2503.21897 year: 2025 --- StreetScape is a modular tactile street puzzle designed to promote spatial reasoning and collaborative play between blind or visually impaired and sighted children. By merging gamified tactile interaction with inclusive design, it reimagines assistive technology as a playful, social, and educational tool.
