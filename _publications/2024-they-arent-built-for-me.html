--- layout: publication authors: - Areen Khalaila - Lane Harrison - Nam Wook Kim - Dylan Cashman arxiv: "2410.08438" html: https://arxiv.org/html/2410.08438v1 pdf: https://arxiv.org/pdf/2410.08438 tags: - accessibility - research - disability - visual impairment - tactile - visualizations title: "They Aren't Built For Me: A Replication Study of Visual Graphical Perception with Tactile Representations of Data for Visually Impaired Users" type: - Paper venue: arXiv venue_tags: - VIS venue_url: https://arxiv.org/abs/2410.08438 year: 2024 --- Our study, inspired by the seminal work of Cleveland and McGill, investigates the perceptual accuracy of blind or visually impaired individuals when interpreting tactile representations of graphical data. By replicating and extending this foundational research, we aim to shed light on the efficacy of tactile displays for conveying complex datasets.
